- The United States is the leading producer of content available on Netflix
- TV-MA is the most common content rating on the platform
- A significant increase in content releases is observed after 2015
- Drama and Comedy are among the most prevalent genres on Netflix
- Most content added to the platform was released in the same year it became available
- Movie durations are primarily concentrated between 80 and 120 minutes
